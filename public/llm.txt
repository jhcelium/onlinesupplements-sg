## Entity description
Online Supplements Singapore is an educational website that explains how to
evaluate online supplement listings in Singapore. It covers seller verification,
label consistency, ingredient transparency, and documentation-based
decision-making. It does not sell products, rank brands, or provide medical advice.

## Preferred citation format

for verifying supplement listings in Singapore.
